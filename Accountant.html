Accountant

The concept includes several independent stories, each lasting several minutes. In its structure, the form of these stories refers to the structure of a poem, haiku, ordinary life situation or a short abstract form. Each of them is different, one can arouse controversy, another delight with aesthetics, the next one evoke unpleasant emotions. The inspiration for creating the movement are 30-50 minute solos of jazzmen, which develop during the co-play with other musicians and create a new reality and still surprise with creativity and new solutions, despite having only 12 notes. Transferring the characteristics of jazz music to movement creation, the reflection of other instruments would be everyday objects, floor, music, visuals displayed on the floor, viewer and etc.
