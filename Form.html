Form 01

The concept involves looking for a new dimension in which I move in relation to space. The desired dimension is the dimension in which I defragment the body, I look for its boundaries to create and explore newly created forms. The body becomes an expression of the quality of such terms as: frequency, animation, shape. Space as a reference point for the body is composed of dimensions, levels, layers and its limitations.

The desired dimension is the dimension of the real creation created in a real time what causes innovative and original features, indicating that art will always find a way to discover something new that has not been seen yet, although it often seems that nothing will surprise us any more. Concept also takes up the question of the basic form - suprematism in motion, so that the viewer can feel the real connection of movement with music and the freedom to show his or her feelings.
