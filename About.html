Damian Drozd

Dancer, choreographer, performer, dance instructor, musician and street art creator in the form of urban lighting. Graduated of Poznań University of Physical Education in the field of Dance in Physical Education. Studied at the Department of Cognitive Science at the Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznań. For several years, he regularly teaches contemporary dance, ballroom dancing, argentino tango, street dance and experimental dance, and creates his own original choreographies and performances. Organizer of events from dance jams, milongas to dance fights to street performances. Saxophonist playing in the Poznań Improvisation Orchestra in Poznań as well as the creator of his own musical productions for his and other projects.

Occasionally clowns and performs on the streets of European cities. Participant of dance fights throughout Europe and ballroom dance tournaments. From 2018-2019 Dancer of the Tango Theater in Poznań, Company of Dance of the University of Physical Education in Poznań, creator of the Rituals project, aimed at connecting dance environments from different sides, so as to blur the line between theater, dance street fighting and performance. Active and regularly representing Poland on the European Experimental Scene e.g. at the Open Your Mind event in Eindhoven with his solo works (2019-2020).
